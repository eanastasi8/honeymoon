Esperienze & Excursions

🍝
Pasta Making
Learn to make fresh pasta from a local nonna — one of the most memorable Tuscany experiences.
🍷
Wine Tasting
Wine tasting in Chianti or Montepulciano — book multiple! Brunello & Vino Nobile are unmissable.
⛵
Amalfi Boat Day
Private or group boat charter along the coast — grottos, swimming, and views of Positano from the water.
📸
Photographer
(Optional) Book a local photographer for a golden-hour session in the hills or along the Amalfi cliffside.
🧗
Praiano Steps Service
Find/book a service to help with luggage on Praiano's famous steep staircase descents.
🏛
Uffizi Gallery
Pre-book tickets! Home to Botticelli's Birth of Venus and rooms of Renaissance masterworks.

Good to know

Tips & Frasi Italiane

✈️
Book flights on Tuesday for the best fares.
⏱
Layover 1–4 hours max — don't cut it too close.
🚂
Use trains for intercity travel — fast, scenic, affordable.
💶
Get Euros at the airport — avoid currency exchange booths.
💳
Choose local currency when card machines ask — avoids bad rates.
💳
No foreign transaction fees — use a card like Chase Sapphire.
📱
Buy an eSIM via Airalo before you leave — cheap data abroad.
💧
Only bottled water — tap water is generally not ideal.
🧳
Pack 80% full — leave room for olive oil, wine, and ceramics.
